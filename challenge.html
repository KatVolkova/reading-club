Reading Challenges

Whatever your interests, preferences and goals are, we can offer you something that can give you food for thoughts, entertainment, evoke emotions, laughter, tears as well as knowledge! Take part in our monthly reading challenges. Every month we offer you our top picks of books in a particular genre including bestsellers and works that are not many are familiar with. Share your thoughts in our community or join our regular meeting in London

Monthly Schedule

  • January-Mystery: Books that involve solving crimes, puzzles, or unraveling enigmatic situations.
  • February- Romance: Stories centered around love, relationships, and emotional connections.
  • March- Science Fiction: Imaginative tales exploring futuristic technology, other worlds, or scientific concepts.
  • April- Fantasy: Books set in magical or fantastical realms, often featuring mythical creatures and heroic quests.
  • May- Historical Fiction: Novels that take place in a specific historical period, incorporating real events and people into fictional stories.
  • June- Thriller/Suspense: Narratives filled with tension, anticipation, and a constant sense of danger or mystery.
  • July - Horror: Books intended to evoke fear, often involving supernatural elements or psychological terror.
  • August-Biography: Accounts of real people's lives, providing insights into their experiences, achievements, and challenges.
  • September- Non-fiction: Books offering factual information on various subjects, such as history, science, philosophy, or self-help.
  • October- Young Adult: Books written specifically for teenage readers, covering a wide range of genres and themes.
  • November- Contemporary Fiction: Novels set in the present time, exploring everyday life, relationships, and societal issues.
  • December- Poetry: Literature characterized by rhythmic and expressive language, emphasizing emotion, imagery, and wordplay.
