Reading Challenges

Whatever your interests, preferences and goals are, we can offer you something that can give you food for thoughts and entertainment, evoke emotions, laughter, and tears, as well as knowledge! Take part in our monthly reading challenges. Every month, we offer you our top picks of books in a particular genre, including bestsellers and works that few are familiar with. Share your thoughts in our community or join our regular meet-ups in London.

Monthly Schedule

  • January - Classical literature
  • February - Romance novels
  • March - Science Fiction
  • April - Fantasy and adventures
  • May - Historical Fiction
  • June - Thriller and Suspense
  • July - Poetry
  • August - Autobiography
  • September - Young Adult
  • October - Horror
  • November - Crime and mystery
  • December - Comedy

Let's meet-up in London!

If you live in London or have a chance to visit this mesmerising City, join our regular meetups where you can encounter avid readers like you and discuss your thoughts on different reading topics. There is a lovely cafe located in one of the largest bookstores in the city.
